If you have an emission point that is within the screening distance of a natural habitat that is either a Special Area of Conservation, Special Protection Area, site of special scientific interest (SSSI) or Ramsar wetlands site, you will need to pay us to do a habitat assessment.

The screening distance for an MCP burning:

  • natural gas or low-sulphur diesel is 5km
  • Biogas, landfill gas or other fuels is 10km

If you have a specified generator you should use the specified generator screening tool to calculate the screening distance for your site.

If you are not sure if there is a relevant habitat within the screening distance of your site, then you can order a free nature and heritage conservation screen (opens new tab).
