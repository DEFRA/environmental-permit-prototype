Existing MCPs operating less than 500 hours per year, as a 5 year rolling average, are exempt from emission limit values (ELV).

New MCPs operating less than 500 hours per year, as a 3 year rolling average, are exempt from meeting ELV. However, a limit of 100 mg/m3 dust applies to biomass MCPs.

MCPs that are also a Part B activity must meet ELV even if the MCP operates for less than 500 hours per year.
